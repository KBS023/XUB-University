Our Programs for Your Career


The School of Computer Science & Engineering is a unique school that leverages the high-quality programs of Xavier University and emphasizes sound computer science fundamentals, coding, research, innovation, and entrepreneurship. It currently offers B.Tech. in Computer Science & Engineering, M. Tech in Data Science & Analytics, and Ph.D in Computer Science and Engineering.
